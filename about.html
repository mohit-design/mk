Hi, I am Mansi!

I am a Personal Stylist and Image Consultant driven by passion, creativity and result. Individual, one-on-one image consultations are my area of expertise. My main objective is to help women succeed by enhancing their appearance for personal or professional needs. Through personalized coaching and style sessions, I give my clients the tools they need to convey expertise and confidence. For the past 6 years, I have worked as a personal stylist and image consultant, servicing women from all walks of life.

I am also a certified trainer and led over 200 hours of corporate training to help participants create the right image, gain confidence by resolving self-esteem/image concerns. I have also done style work for some of the top corporates. Additionally, I have experience of styling brand advertisements.

I also hold a certificate in fashion design which is useful when creating bespoke garments for clients or explaining the construction of garments.

Everyone has their own unique style and it's my job as a stylist to amp it up. The goal of my styling services is to save you time, teach you how to shop smarter and rekindle your love of dressing up. I take great satisfaction in offering a highly personalized service with a laser-like focus on your unique demands. Being in Dubai, I am able to work on projects of any scale, whether they are high-end or mainstream.

Mansi Khatri is a Dubai based Personal Stylist, image consultant who helps women reinvent their style and identity.
