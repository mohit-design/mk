Mansi will help you share your narrative with the world, whether you're wanting to update your wardrobe, require an outfit for a special occasion, or discover your style from top to bottom.

You've spent a lot of time and money shopping for yourself, only to return to a closet that doesn't suit your requirements. The pieces don't go with each other or you tend to wear the same outfits that you've outgrown. Together, let's do something magical about that!

Wardrobe Revamp

Do you want to give your wardrobe a thorough makeover and identify your personal style because you're sick of feeling annoyed staring at your clustered wardrobe in the mornings?

This is a perfect option if you need help organizing your wardrobe. We will make sure it reflects your personal style along with functional needs and leave you with knowledge of how to make the most of your wardrobe.

Who is this for?

  • Every morning you stand in front of your wardrobe full of clothes and you feel as if you have nothing to wear.
  • You think getting ready for the day is a tedious and burdensome task.
  • Maybe you've bought something thinking it looked good on someone else, only to be disappointed when you realized it didn't go with the rest of your wardrobe.
  • Maybe you find yourself trying to stay up with trends, but all you wind up doing is wearing things once or never.
  • You can also be clinging to things you no longer wear or might be hoarding things because of the associations you have made with them, even though they no longer serve you and are only taking up room.

Process

  • An online style consultation to better understand you and your style goals.
  • We try on items in your closet to see what needs an update or no longer fits. We'll get rid of the parts that aren't helping you anymore while keeping the ones that are.
  • We will demonstrate how to create impactful outfits and accessorize them from your freshly organized wardrobe. We will document the outfits by clicking pictures. We will also identify a wardrobe gap with a list of products that will enable you to shop purposefully in the future.
  • After your session, you can get email-based direct stylist support.

Personal Shopping & Styling

You have no idea who or what "you" are. You have no idea what your personal style is, and you feel out of control. We'll assist you in defining your personal style so you can comfortably browse for items that reflect your personality, lifestyle and goals.

Who is this for?

  • You don't want to shop, or perhaps you used to like it, but you don't know what stores to go to anymore or what styles are best for your needs and body shape.
  • Your closet may appear full or empty but it is missing key items and the pieces you have no longer resonate with you or your style.
  • You'd want some new ideas and advice on how to mix your current wardrobe pieces with new items that are authentic to “yourself” while also making them look better and more coordinated.

Process

  • A 1 hour online style consultation to better understand you and your style goals.
  • A virtual wardrobe review will help us determine what you currently own and which items are needed to complete your wardrobe.
  • Before your shopping session begins, your stylist will first hit the stores and pre-select items from your shopping list so that you just have to try on outfits that are personalized to your brief. In case of an online consultation, we will send you the links of the outfits as per the country you are based in.
  • We'll teach you important information about what to look for in clothing to enhance your shape, complement your lifestyle, elevate your own style, and generate versatility in each item you buy throughout your shopping session.
  • We'll demonstrate how to incorporate your new purchases into your existing wardrobe. Your wardrobe will be filled with outfits curated as per your needs and when you wear them and look at yourself in the mirror it will spark joy.
  • A follow-up session with your stylist after you wear your new outfits.

The Complete Style Makeover

No more "what should I wear?" "I'm not sure whether that's my style."

The Complete Experience, as the name suggests, will give you not only a 360-degree styling experience but also clarity and confidence.

The best possible transformational experience to help you level up and present your best self.

Who is this for?

  • You are ready to undergo 360 degree styling experience.
  • You want to feel confident in all aspects of your life.
  • You need to update your clothing because you're feeling stale and uninspired.
  • You feel insecure wearing the clothes you've chosen to wear.
  • You are looking for new personal branding.
  • You are prepared to improve your personal style and appearance with new, trendy items.

Process

  • 01

    Style Consultation

    A thorough evaluation that includes identifying your body type, color, lifestyle, and taste. We will also establish your style goals and clarify your vision!

  • 02

    Wardrobe Evaluation

    We will build a wardrobe of your dreams by having a fresh perspective and a new set of eyes. We will assess, declutter, categorize and organize your wardrobe. We will create impactful looks from your wardrobe and identify a wardrobe gap.

  • 03

    Personal Shopping

    Once your shopping list is complete, we'll offer suggestions and walk you through the process to make shopping easy. We will provide you with outfit links of brands that suit your budget and style or we will go visit stores to shop.

  • 04

    Styling Session

    We'll incorporate your new pieces into your wardrobe as soon as they arrive to give you countless outfit alternatives. You'll learn about our mix-and-match method to maximize your outfits as well as several styling advice and techniques that will make you look great.

  • 05

    Personalized Style Summary

    We will give you a summary of all your sessions along with some tips. You will also receive your Digital Lookbook and recommendation for your next step.

  • 06

    Further Style Support

    A check in call with the stylist post trying your new outfits. We'll be happy to address your style related queries.

Event / Holiday Styling

Allow us to dress you head to toe so you are prepared for your upcoming memorable event, whether it be an engagement shoot, a personal branding session, a keynote speech, or an interview for the job of your dreams. We'll help you choose the ideal clothing for that particular event and make sure you look and feel like your most authentic self on your special day.

Who is this for?

  • A major event is coming up, and you want to be the greatest version of yourself possible in addition to looking and feeling amazing. You want to make the most of the opportunity.

Process

  • A style consultation that involves a one-on-one interview to learn everything about you and your goals, your taste, the brands you like for the event.
  • After that, we will work with you to choose the best outfit(s) for the event
  • You will receive assistance at every stage, even with the slightest of details like how you pair your shoes and accessories.
